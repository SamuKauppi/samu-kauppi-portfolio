About Me

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio. My name is Samu Kauppi, and I am 27-year-old Finnish student with extensive experience in coding and with 2D/3D-graphics. Over the 5 years that I have been coding, I have worked on multiple projects from which I have gathered various skills and lot of knowledge. In addition to my personal projects, I completed an apprenticeship at Niemikotisäätiö and earned my degree in Information and Communication Technologies from Business College Helsinki. My primary job for Niemikotisäätiö was to create a 3D simulation of their workplace in Unity. I also taught 3D modelling their clients.

2019 I completed my conscript service for the Finnish army in Hamina army academy. There I worked as a virtual support person. The jobs entailed developing the Finnish army virtual battle space which is a mod for a game called Arma. I also taught other conscripts on how to work effectively in virtual space.

After army I completed game studies at HEO. In HEO I learned lot of valuable game and graphic techniques that I utilize to this day. Game engines like Unity and Construct 3, as well as image editing tools like Photoshop, GIMP, and Clip Studio Paint, are examples.

Currently I study at Metropolia University of Applied Sciences. I started my studies in 2022, and I have improved in coding a lot since then. Both learning new languages like Python, JavaScript and C++ and improving a lot with Unity.

Since my childhood I have been interested in games and computers, making this career path natural for me. Outside of computer software my hobbies include swimming and going outdoors. I often go skiing during winter, while spring and autumn are the best times for fishing. I also love travelling because they always end up being unforgettable trips. Some of the best trips I have experienced have been my skiing trip to Japan and my summer trip to Singapore. I also enjoy playing board games with my friends. Currently I am obsessed by Yu-Gi-Oh and Riichi Mahjong.

I am a hard worker, passionate and I excel working alone, but also thrive in teamwork. I start proactively working on projects because I have a strong self-discipline and I am confident in my talents as a software developer. I have a sense of humour and I love meeting new people. Every time I meet a new person, I learn new things.
